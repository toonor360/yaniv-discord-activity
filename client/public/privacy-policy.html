Yaniv Discord Activity Privacy Policy

This page outlines how the Yaniv Discord Activity website ( yaniv-discord-activity-front.onrender.com ) and the Yaniv Discord Activity use your data.

Effective Date: October 27, 2024

Data Collected

Yaniv Discord Activity does not share data with data brokers or advertisers.

Yaniv Discord Activity may record your IP address and Discord account ID in order to prevent abuse. This data will generally not be shared but may be provided to law enforcement or other third parties in the event of extremely abusive or illegal behavior, or to protect the safety of users.

When accessed through Discord, Yaniv Discord Activity requires a small amount of information about your Discord account in order to function. This includes your account ID, account name, and the servers and channels you are a member of. If you access Yaniv through Discord, you may want to familiarize yourself with their privacy policy.

Security

Yaniv Discord Activity takes reasonable measures to ensure your data is secure.

Cookies

Yaniv Discord Activity does not use cookies.

Minors

Minors under the age of 13 are not permitted to use Yaniv Discord Activity.

Changes

This document may change from time to time. Users will be provided with at least 1 week of notice of any changes.
